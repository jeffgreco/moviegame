How to Play

Think you know your film history? Put down your Cahiers du Cinemas and get to work.

You'll be shown a series of movies. Place each one on the timeline in the correct order by initial US release date. Drag it into position, or tap a + to drop it there. One point per correct placement! Remakes or other movies with identical titles will list their director as well.

In Daily mode, everyone gets the same set of films. Come back tomorrow for a new challenge.

Random mode draws from over 1,000 movies for unlimited play.

Challenge lets you take a great score on Random and challenge your friends with the same run.

Found an error or bug? We bet. Let us know.
