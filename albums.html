How to Play

Think you know your music history? Time to prove it.

You'll be shown a series of classic albums from Rolling Stone's 500 Greatest Albums list. Place each one on the timeline in the correct order by release date. Drag it into position, or tap a + to drop it there. One point per correct placement! Albums with the same title will show the artist name.

In Daily mode, everyone gets the same themed set of albums. Come back tomorrow for a new challenge. Random mode draws from 250 classic albums for unlimited play.
